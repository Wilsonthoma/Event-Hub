Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventHub.

How do I book tickets for an event?
Simply browse events, select your preferred ticket type, choose quantity, and complete the payment. You'll receive a QR code ticket via email.
How do I become an organizer?
Click "Become an Organizer" on the homepage, register with your organization details, and start creating events.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept M-Pesa, Credit/Debit cards, and PayPal.
How do I get a refund?
Contact our support team within 7 days of purchase. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
How do I contact customer support?
Email us at support@eventhub.com or call +254 700 123 456.
